How to Apply:
To fill online application form, click on the link “APPLY ONLINE” to register for the program and create password for the application process. Do not share the password with anybody.
Eligible    candidates   must    complete   and    submit    the    application   form    online only [link available on the Institute website www.bitmesra.ac.in].
Pay   application fee    of Rs.1,500/- (General / OBC candidates) and  Rs.1,000/- (SC/ST candidates) through Online Payment Gateway (candidates  may use Net banking / Debit card / Credit card of any nationalized bank) till the last date of application process.
Note: Fees once remitted shall not be refunded under any circumstances.
