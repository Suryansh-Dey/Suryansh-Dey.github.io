Eligibility Criteria and other conditions:
- Candidates applying for B.Sc. Chemistry (Honors/Honors with Research) must have passed Class 12/ I.Sc. or Equivalent Qualifying Examination with English, Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics/Biology as major subject with a minimum 60% marks (50% for SC/ST/PwD).
- Candidates who have already completed 4 Yr. B.Sc. Chemistry (Honors with Research) applying for M.Sc. Chemistry (Research) from other institutes must have 55% marks (50% marks for SC/ST/PwD) from a recognized University.

Notes:
- Only those candidates whose date of birth falls on or after 1 October 1999 in case of General category and 1 October 1993 in case of SC/ST/PwD are eligible for admission in B.Sc. Chemistry (Honors/Honors with Research). Date of Birth as recorded in the Secondary Education Board Class X (High School) certificate only will be considered as authentic.
- Candidates with foreign diplomas should see the EdCIL website for equivalence (http://www.edcil.co.in), and if required should obtain an equivalence certificate from the evaluation division of the AIU (website http://www.aiuweb.org).
- In case any Board awards grades instead of marks, the calculation of equivalent marks will be based on the procedure prescribed by the Board.
- At any level of the studies [Class X or Class XII], a candidate / applicant must have passed all the required subjects at that level from the same Board
